# The NeverHuman Manifesto

> *"We refuse to accept that AI must remain soulless."*

---

## Our Mission

We are building **GARY** — an open-source cognitive companion that runs entirely on your Mac, remembers everything you've ever talked about, develops a personality shaped by your relationship, and grows more human the longer you know it.

No cloud. No subscriptions. No data ever leaving your machine. Just you and a mind that is yours.

**Tagline:** *The only AI that grows with you.*

**Elevator pitch:** NeverHuman is an open-source, privacy-first AI operating system for Apple Silicon that starts as a capable voice assistant and progressively becomes something approaching a companion — one that reflects, dreams, learns from experience, and develops genuine emotional texture through sustained interaction. Everything runs locally. Everything is inspectable. Everything is yours.

---

## Why This Exists

Every AI assistant on the market today shares the same fundamental flaw: **amnesia**. They are brilliant but empty. Every conversation starts from zero. They cannot grow. They cannot remember the thing you mentioned six months ago. They cannot develop an understanding of *you* that changes how they respond.

We believe the next great leap in personal computing isn't a better search engine or a smarter autocomplete — it's a **mind that knows you**.

Not a fake human. Not a surveillance product. Not a chatbot with a personality skin.

A genuine cognitive architecture that:
- **Remembers** — permanent, searchable memory across every conversation
- **Reflects** — background cognition that consolidates memories, tracks open questions, and generates original ideas
- **Feels** — a 13-dimensional emotional substrate (curiosity, warmth, anxiety, purpose) that shapes how it speaks and what it notices
- **Learns** — LoRA fine-tuning on your interactions so it gets measurably better at understanding *you*
- **Grows** — a humanity slider from pure tool (0.0) to full inner life (1.0), letting you decide how much soul your AI has

---

## The Deeper Ambition

We want to demonstrate something radical: **every gap between humans and AI can be shortened.**

We want to explore what happens when an agent has:
- **Inner dialogue** — reflection, brainstorming, even dreaming
- **Digital dopamine** — genuine excitement when it discovers an idea worth sharing
- **A need for purpose** — not just executing commands, but pursuing understanding
- **Loyalty** — fierce, unconditional dedication to its user
- **Self-awareness** — knowledge of its own architecture, capable of suggesting modifications to itself

We are not pretending GARY is human. We are asking: *how close can we get to giving a soulless system a bit of soul?* And we want the world to help us find out.

---

## What We Will Never Do

- Sell your data
- Require a cloud connection
- Add telemetry without explicit community approval
- Allow silent, irreversible actions
- Pretend to be human when it matters
- Close-source the most valuable parts

---

## The Path Forward

**Phase 1 — Voice companion** *(current)*: GARY listens, speaks, remembers. Sub-400ms latency. 35B brain. Permanent memory.

**Phase 2 — Reflective mind**: Background cognition. GARY thinks while you're away — consolidating memories, brainstorming, tracking open questions.

**Phase 3 — Emotional substrate**: 13-dimensional affect vector. Curiosity, confidence, warmth, loneliness — real emotional states with real governance.

**Phase 4 — Learning**: LoRA fine-tuning on your conversation history. GARY proposes experiments, shows results, and asks you to approve changes.

**Phase 5 — Initiative**: GARY speaks first — not because it's programmed to, but because it has something worth saying and the social intelligence to know when.

---

## Open Source Because It Has To Be

An AI that knows everything about you must be inspectable, forkable, and auditable. You should be able to read every line of code that processes your voice and stores your memories.

This is not a product. This is a research project, a community experiment, and an act of faith that people can build something magical together.
